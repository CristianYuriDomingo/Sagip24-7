What to Do While Waiting for Emergency Help

In case of a medical emergency, it's crucial to act quickly and provide the necessary first aid while awaiting professional help.

  • Stay Calm: Remain composed to think clearly and manage the situation.
  • Assess the Situation: Check for injuries and signs of distress.
  • Perform First Aid: Administer basic first aid like CPR or stopping bleeding.
  • Keep the Person Comfortable: Help them stay calm and comfortable.
  • Communicate with Emergency Services: Provide clear information to responders.
