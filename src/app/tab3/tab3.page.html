About more... FAQs

Find answers to frequently asked questions here.

What should I do if I encounter an emergency?

If you encounter a medical emergency, follow these steps:

  • Ensure the area is safe for both you and the injured person.
  • Call emergency services if necessary.
  • Use the app to find relevant first aid steps offline based on the type of emergency.
How can I use the app without an internet connection?

The SAGIP 24/7 app works offline. It has a local database of essential first aid instructions that you can access even without an internet connection. Just open the app, choose the emergency type, and follow the steps.

Can I trust the medical advice in the app?

Yes, the first aid information in the app is based on trusted medical guidelines and protocols. However, in severe cases, always contact medical professionals for immediate help.

Must Have First Aid Kit

Ensure you have a well-stocked first aid kit for emergencies. Here's a list of essential items to keep in your kit:

  • Bandages (various sizes)
  • Antiseptic wipes
  • Adhesive tape
  • Gauze pads
  • Scissors
  • Thermometer
  • Pain relievers
